
Controls:
    Draw lines			Right mouse button
    Draw boxes			Left mouse button
    Draw the goal		A
    Dismiss the goal		S
    Shoot cannon ball		SPACE
    
Extra features:
	To add power to the shoot hold SPACE.
	When a cannon ball hits the target you earn a single gold bar. When you have earned five gold bars,
	they get traded up for a valuable red diamond! COLLECT THEM :D


Also, pleas remember to turn up the volume;)
